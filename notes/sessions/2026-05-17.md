## Interview Qs the layers code generates (sampler)

If you only read these, here are 5 questions to defend at depth:

1. **Why divide attention scores by √d_k?** (variance argument; softmax
   saturation at large d_k)
2. **Why fp32 for RMSNorm's mean-of-squares when training is bf16?**
   (bf16 mantissa loses precision when summing d_model squared values)
3. **Why does RoPE give relative positions?**
   (rotations commute through inner products as difference of angles)
4. **What's the difference between BPE and WordPiece?**
   (greedy-most-frequent vs likelihood-maximizing; both produce similar
   results)
5. **What's the load-balancing aux loss in MoE for? Why N · Σ(f·P) and
   not just Σ(f·log f)?**
   (prevents router collapse; the f·P formulation gives a gradient even
   when one expert dominates, entropy form does not)

Full answers for each are in `agent_implementation_log.md`.
